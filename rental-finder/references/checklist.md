# Rental Search Checklist

## Need-to-know inputs
- city
- dates
- monthly or nightly stay
- budget ceiling
- entire place or room
- solo or shared
- furnished?
- kitchen?
- washer?
- workspace?
- quiet or nightlife?

## Compare options on
- total cost, not headline price only
- review count and rating
- exact bed setup
- internet/workspace quality
- washer/kitchen availability
- AC or ventilation if relevant
- transport and walkability
- gym proximity if important
- noise warnings

## Good listing signals
- 4.6+ rating when enough reviews exist
- 20+ reviews for trust, or strong host profile if fewer
- clear photos of bathroom/kitchen/bed
- self-check-in for flexibility
- monthly discount shown
- strong wifi mention

## Bad signals
- very few details
- suspiciously low price
- unclear fees
- noisy location for a work-focused stay
- host asks to pay off-platform

## Recommendation style
When ranking, explicitly say:
- best overall fit
- best budget pick
- best zone pick
- major downside of each
