# Workflow

## Minimal single-image video
1. Take one image.
2. Take one audio file.
3. Loop the image visually for the full audio duration.
4. Encode to MP4.

## Multi-image slideshow
1. Collect image order.
2. Decide whether images are evenly timed or custom timed.
3. Add the audio track.
4. Export as MP4.

## Optional enhancements
- title card
- fade in/out
- subtitles later
- simple text overlays
- vertical framing
- multiple-image sequencing

## Recommended implementation order
1. vertical preset
2. multiple-image slideshow
3. title overlay

## Delivery mindset
Prioritize a render that is easy to upload and share over fancy editing.
