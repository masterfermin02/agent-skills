# Fitness Review Workflow

## Trigger
Daily recap, weekly review, meal/workout tracking, or body progress check-in.

## Steps
1. Query SQLite fitness tracker.
2. Summarize calories, protein, workouts, and trend quality.
3. Compare against current targets.
4. Give direct coaching feedback.
5. Keep recommendations short and actionable.

## Output
- totals
- target gap
- day score / week score
- main improvement lever
